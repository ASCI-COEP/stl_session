Given an array of integers, find the element with the highest frequency.

INPUT FORMAT:
First line of input consists of an integer 'n', denoting the size of array
Next line of the input consists of 'n' space separated integers denoting the array

OUTPUT FORMAT:
For each input, output the element with maximum frequency.

Eg.

INPUT:
10
4 12 123 32 12 123 9 12 9 4

OUTPUT:
12

Explanation:

Frequency Count:
4 - 2
9 - 2
12 - 3
32 - 1
123 - 2
